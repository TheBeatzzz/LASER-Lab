An All-in-One Solution

Our system integrates cutting-edge technologies to provide a comprehensive workflow from diagnosis to treatment.

Advanced 3D OCT Imaging

Utilizes non-harmful, near-infrared light to create high-resolution, cross-sectional 3D images of skin layers. Visualize lesion roots, collagen density, and skin thickness without invasive biopsies.

Targeted Photodynamic Therapy

Delivers precise, light-activated treatment using a proprietary Aza-BODIPY photosensitizer. Effectively targets abnormal cells and bacteria for acne, warts, and skin cancer with minimal side effects.

AI-Powered Diagnostics

Our U-Net deep learning model automatically segments skin layers and identifies abnormalities from OCT scans. Provides objective, quantitative data to support clinical decisions and track treatment efficacy.

Ergonomic Handheld Probe

A lightweight, user-friendly handheld probe integrates both imaging and therapy delivery systems, streamlining the clinical workflow and enhancing patient comfort during procedures.

Simple, Powerful Workflow

From initial scan to targeted therapy in three seamless steps.

1

Scan & Image

Perform a quick, non-invasive 3D scan of the target area with the handheld probe to instantly capture detailed subsurface images.

2

Analyze with AI

AI-powered software analyzes the OCT data in real-time, segmenting layers and highlighting abnormalities for a precise diagnosis.

3

Deliver Targeted Therapy

Based on the AI-assisted diagnosis, use the same device to deliver focused Photodynamic Therapy directly to the affected tissue.
